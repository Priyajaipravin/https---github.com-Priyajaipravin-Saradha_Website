About Us

Sharadha Skill Academy (SSA) is a renowned national-level skill-building program initiative launched by KG Group of companies, under the aegis of Sri Palani Murugan Trust, with the biggest dream of providing job-oriented vocational skills to underprivileged youth for improving their social, economic, quality of life.Sharadha Skill Academy offers vocational training for 12 weeks, which concentrates majorly on providing industry-relevant, practical training on a pro-bono basis to make the youth employable and self-dependant in this competitive market. The training focuses on building life skills that include effective communication, etiquette, and grooming. Academy has tied up with industry leaders as employment partners to create employment for the candidates.
